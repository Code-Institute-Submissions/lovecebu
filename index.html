Cebu... Queen City of the South!



It's More Fun In Cebu!

Cebu is the oldest city in the Philippines, after it was First discovered by the Spaniards and the Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan in 1521. It became the First Christian island in the Philippines and the First settlement of the Spaniards.

Fast forward... Cebu is the 2nd largest city in the Philippines, (Manila being the Capital city) and is one of the most visited in Asia, by both international and local visitors. From North to South, Cebu has a lot to offer.

We are looking forward to having you here in Cebu!
